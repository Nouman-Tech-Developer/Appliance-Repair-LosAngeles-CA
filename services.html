Kitchen & Laundry Appliances We Repair

We repair kitchen and laundry appliances used every day in homes and businesses.

Refrigerator Repair

A refrigerator that is not cooling can spoil your food. We check the cooling system and fix the part causing the problem. Proper cooling is restored to keep your food fresh.

Freezer Repair

Heavy frost or weak cooling can affect frozen food. We inspect the main parts and restore the right temperature. This helps the freezer maintain steady cooling.

Ice Maker Repair

An ice maker may stop making ice or begin leaking water. We check the water line and repair the faulty part. The unit is tested for normal ice production.

Wine Cooler Repair

Changing temperatures can affect your wine collection. We inspect the cooling system and bring back steady cooling. This helps maintain the right storage temperature.

Oven Repair

An oven that does not heat properly can delay cooking. We check the heating parts and restore even heat. The oven is tested to make sure it cooks properly.

Range & Stove Repair

Burners that do not light can make cooking difficult. We repair the ignition system and restore safe heating. Each burner is checked for steady performance.

Cooktop Repair

A cooktop may stop heating due to a power issue. We check the controls and repair the damaged part. This brings back safety and even heating.

Microwave Repair

A microwave may run without heating the food. We inspect the main parts and fix the heating issue. The controls are also checked for proper use.

Range Hood Repair

A weak fan may fail to remove smoke from the kitchen. We repair the fan and restore proper airflow. This helps keep the kitchen air clean.

Dishwasher Repair

A dishwasher that does not drain can leave dirty water inside. We clear the blockage and repair the drain system. The unit is tested through a complete wash cycle.

Washer Repair

A washer may stop spinning or draining during a cycle. We check the pump and moving parts to restore normal use. This helps the washer complete each cycle properly.

Dryer Repair

A dryer that does not heat can leave clothes wet. We inspect the heating system and restore proper drying. The airflow is also checked for faster drying.

Garbage Disposal Repair

A blocked disposal can stop the sink from draining. We clear the jam and repair the unit so it works smoothly. The drain flow is checked before the service ends.
