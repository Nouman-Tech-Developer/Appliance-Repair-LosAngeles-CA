Common Appliance Problems

Q13 What should I do if my appliance suddenly stops working?
First, check the power supply to make sure the unit is plugged in, and the circuit breaker hasn't tripped. If the power connection is fine and it still won't turn on, it is best to call a professional for appliance repair. We can diagnose the issue quickly and safely.
Q14 What are the common signs that an appliance needs repair?
Look out for strange noises, unusual smells, water leaks, or a sudden drop in performance. We handle everything from refrigerator and freezer repair, ice maker and wine cooler repair, to oven and range repair, stove and cooktop repair, washer and dryer repair, and dishwasher repair.
Q15 Can you fix refrigerators that are not cooling properly?
Yes, we can. A fridge that will not stay cold is a very common appliance problem that can quickly lead to spoiled food. Our technicians are highly trained to find the issue and get your unit cooling again.
Q16 Do you repair leaking dishwashers or washing machines?
Absolutely. Leaks can cause serious water damage to your floors and home, so it is important to fix them fast. We provide expert washer and dryer repair, as well as dishwasher repair, to stop leaks and get your laundry and kitchen routines back to normal.

Maintenance & Preparation

Q18 How can I extend the life of my home appliances?
Regular cleaning and basic upkeep are the best ways to keep your machines running well. Make sure to clean your filters, check hoses for leaks, and avoid overloading your washer or dishwasher. Scheduling routine maintenance with a professional can also help catch small issues before they turn into costly breakdowns.
Q19 What should I do before the technician arrives for repair?
Please clear the space around the appliance so our technician has plenty of room to work. It is also helpful to empty the unit if we are working on a fridge or oven, and please secure any pets in another room. Having a clear path and a brief description of the problem ready will help us get the job done faster.
Q20 Why should I hire a professional appliance repair technician?
Hiring a professional ensures the repair is done safely and correctly the first time. Modern appliances use complex electrical and water systems that can be dangerous to work on without the right training. Our skilled technicians have the proper tools and experience to quickly fix your appliances, saving you time, money, and stress in the long run.
